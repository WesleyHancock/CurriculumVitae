Boom fitness logo

Boom Fitness, a prominent health and fitness brand in South Africa, has established itself as a leader in the industry with an impressive following of over 35,000 Instagram enthusiasts. As their graphic designer, I had the privilege of creating their logo.

To embody the bold and impactful nature of Boom Fitness, I opted for a logo design that exudes strength and energy. I carefully selected a thick typeface with rigid edges, allowing the typography to make a strong statement. The robust letterforms create a sense of solidity and determination, reflecting the brand's commitment to helping individuals achieve their fitness goals.

In order to infuse an additional layer of dynamism and symbolism into the logo, I incorporated a clever visual element. By leveraging the negative space created by the joining of the two 'o's in the word "boom," I artfully crafted a lightning bolt shape. This integration not only adds an exciting visual element but also represents the explosive energy and rapid progress that individuals can experience through their fitness journey with Boom Fitness.

Wheelhouse logo

Wheelhouse, a renowned parent company hosting a diverse portfolio of brands and businesses, required a logo that encapsulated the grandeur of its presence. As their graphic designer, I was tasked with creating a visual identity that harmoniously merged the majestic Burj Khalifa with the symbol of control embodied by the helm of a ship. The result is a logo that seamlessly blends beauty and order, sophistication and guidance.

Drawing inspiration from the Burj Khalifa, an iconic architectural marvel known for its awe-inspiring height and elegance, I incorporated its distinctive silhouette into the logo design. The sleek lines and graceful curves of the Burj Khalifa represent the beauty and refinement that Wheelhouse exudes as a parent company.

To convey the sense of control and strategic guidance that Wheelhouse provides to its portfolio of brands, I integrated the symbol of a helm—a traditional navigational tool used to steer ships. The helm symbolizes the firm hand that guides and steers businesses towards success, ensuring a steady and purposeful course.

By fusing these two elements—the Burj Khalifa and the helm—I created a logo that harmonizes the majesty of architecture with the symbolism of control.
